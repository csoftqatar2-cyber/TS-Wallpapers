TS Wallpapers turns a car head unit, tablet or TV into a live wallpaper display
with an elegant fullscreen clock overlay.

Wallpapers
- slideshow of photos, animated GIFs and videos (MP4 and more)
- swipe or use the remote's left/right keys to switch; auto-switch every minute
- wallpapers are managed remotely: add or remove an image once and every
  activated device receives the change automatically
- per-device wallpapers: assign private images to a single car while global
  images reach all cars
- add local media from the device, or pair a phone by QR code and upload
  over Wi-Fi
- videos are pre-downloaded so playback loops smoothly and works offline

Clock
- analog and digital clock, individually configurable (position, size, fonts,
  colors, show/hide)
- date view with Hijri calendar and Arabic digit support
- weather, battery, alarms, calendar events and notification count overlays
- burn-in protection for OLED screens
- usable as Android screensaver (DayDream) and as home-screen widgets
- compatible with Android TV / car head units (16:9, remote-friendly)

The app self-updates: new versions are offered in-app, no store needed.
